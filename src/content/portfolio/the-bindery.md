Imagine a home with every modern convenience that also pays homage to New York City’s storied past. Welcome to the Bindery where elements of classic design meet the latest residential features. In the early 1900’s, Long Island City was a center of the book binding business and that rich industrial history is the inspiration for a lifestyle that honors yesterday while offering a solid foundation of comfort for your everyday.
